# Mobile App Ideas - Brainstorming Document
## Step 1: New App Ideas - List
### 1. Galactic Databank (Star Wars Lore App)
A comprehensive Star Wars encyclopedia app that allows users to explore characters, vehicles, species, locations, droids, creatures, and organizations from the entire Star Wars universe using the official Star Wars Databank API. Users can search, browse by category, bookmark favorites, and discover detailed lore about any element of the saga.
### 2. Recipe Remix
A cooking app that helps users create recipes based on ingredients they already have at home. Users can input available ingredients, set dietary restrictions, and get AI-generated recipe suggestions. Includes step-by-step cooking instructions, timer features, and ability to save favorite combinations.
### 3. Study Buddy Tracker
A productivity app designed for students to track study sessions, create flashcard decks, set study goals, and analyze their learning patterns. Includes Pomodoro timer, progress analytics, and the ability to share study materials with classmates.
### 4. Fitness Challenge Hub
A social fitness app where users can create and join fitness challenges with friends. Track workouts, share progress photos, compete on leaderboards, and earn badges. Integrates with health apps and includes custom challenge creation.
### 5. Local Event Finder
A location-based app that shows nearby events, concerts, meetups, and activities happening in real-time. Users can filter by interest categories, RSVP to events, meet other attendees, and discover hidden gems in their area.
### 6. Budget Buddy
A personal finance tracker that helps users manage expenses, set savings goals, and visualize spending patterns. Features include receipt scanning, bill reminders, category-based budgeting, and financial health scores.

## Step 3: Final Decision
### Selected App: Galactic Databank (Star Wars Lore App)
This app scored highest overall (23/25) and best satisfies the evaluation criteria. Here's why it's the winner:
#### Strengths:
•	Universal appeal - Star Wars has a massive, passionate fanbase worldwide
•	High engagement potential - Users will reference it while watching content, making it genuinely useful
•	Clear technical scope - Using an existing API keeps the project manageable and realistic
•	Strong story - Carrying the entire Star Wars universe in your pocket is exciting
•	Excellent market size - Millions of potential users across all demographics
•	Perfect for mobile - Quick reference lookups are ideal for mobile format
•	Achievable timeline - Core features can be built within capstone constraints
#### Why it beats the alternatives:
•	More achievable than Fitness Challenge Hub (no backend/social features needed)
•	More universally appealing than Study Buddy (not limited to students)
•	Better defined than Local Event Finder (no need for event APIs or location permissions)
•	More engaging than Budget Buddy (entertainment vs. chores)
•	Existing API provides rich, professional content (vs. Recipe Remix requiring recipe generation)
The app will serve as the go-to mobile reference for Star Wars lore, making it easy to look up any character, vehicle, species, location, droid, creature, or organization on-the-go. It solves the real problem of wanting instant answers about Star Wars content while watching movies/shows or during fan discussions.
